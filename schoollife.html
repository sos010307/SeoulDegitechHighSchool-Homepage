2019 GLOBAL FIELD LEARNING PROJECT IN SEOUL DIGITECH HIGH SCHOOL

The selection areas for global field learning at Seoul Digital High School are Future Growth, Global market entry and Overseas Experience. Seoul Digital High School recruits students who are interested in each field and cultivates them systematically. In addition, it provides an effective program to enhance not only its global capabilities but also its professional working capacity by strengthening the connection of field learning in various overseas environments. What students get in the global field learning is not just specs, but more fundamental growth, encountering foreign cultures, philosophies and other aspects of themselves.
